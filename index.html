About Us

TK Propeller Digital Solutions was started as a completely “ out-of- garage” company with at most customer satisfaction as our core belief. As the customer and project size grew, we made it official to the world that there is a new kid in the digital block. We are primarily a service oriented company and we are slowly focusing on product building too. We are bunch of young minds who want to make some kick-ass web applications and products which will make a significant impact in our customers’ day-to-day operations. We especially provide affordable services and products to MSMEs as we live and breathe the same air as them.
